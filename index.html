How to use it

Step 1

Open up Spotify and go to the playlist you want to compare.

Step 2

Click on a random song within your playlist. This will mark the song you clicked on.

Step 3

Press Ctrl+A to mark every song on the playlist. Now press Ctrl+C to copy all of them. The last step is to press Ctrl+V in the input field on this website.
